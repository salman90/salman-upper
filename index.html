Who we are and what we do.

Uppercase is an integrated software provider that offers a range of solutions to clients seeking web development, design, and visual branding. At Uppercase, we listen, advise, and guide our clients through the creation process to ensure that your desired virtual identity is created.

Web Development

From websites and web pages to electronic applications, we develop functional software to create efficient user interfaces. Our Web and Content Management System ensures that products are scalable, content is easily editable, and more, in order to provide our clients and their users with unparalleled efficiency.

Web Design

To match our strong backend skills, we establish the right aesthetic for each website or application and integrate it with the right interactive features in order to best deliver pages to users. Our responsive designs accomodate multifaceted products that are compatible with electronic devices of various types and sizes.

Branding Design

Imagery is an important feature of today’s increasingly visual world. Our designers possess the artistic sensibility and acumen required to create iconic logos in order to build a solid brand identity for our clients. We also provide graphic design for print and social media to help tell a brand’s story across multiple platforms.
